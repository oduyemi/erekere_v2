What’s Erekere About?

Erekere is a Nigerian tour operating company built to promote tourism, deepen cultural appreciation, and connect people through unforgettable experiences. We organise group tours, school excursions, corporate retreats, holiday packages, and guided experiences that reveal the beauty, diversity, and richness of Nigeria.

Our Mission

  • To be a trusted tourism brand delivering premium experiences at accessible prices.
  • To create greater awareness of the beauty, culture, and richness of Nigeria.

Our Vision

  • To curate travel experiences that linger for both individuals and groups.
  • To inspire deeper appreciation for Nigeria through stronger domestic tourism and cultural enlightenment.
